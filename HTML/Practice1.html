My First Travel Blog

It was 13th January 2023 me with my family had gone to the Saint Martin island.
It is the only coral island in Bangladesh.We started our journey in the evening of 12 January by bus. We reached the Cox's Bazar Ship point in the early morning .Then we started towards Saint Martin by Ship. We reached their in the noon. It was a place with lot of scenic beauty. We were so amazed after seeing the clear sea water & fishes were playing in that water. We had eaten fish bbq in the evening & we had a chicken bbq party there. We came back to our home on 15th January after a journey of 2 days.The travel to Saint Martin peeps into my mind.
